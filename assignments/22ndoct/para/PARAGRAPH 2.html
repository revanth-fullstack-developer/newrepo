GLOBAL WARMING

Global warming is the long-term
heating of Earth's climate system observed since
the pre-industrial period (between 1850 and 1900) due to human activities, primarily fossil fuel burning, which increases heat-trapping greenhouse gas levels in Earth's atmosphere.
